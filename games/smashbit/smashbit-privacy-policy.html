Hi! Thanks for playing Smashbit.

I (SmileLulz, the developer) care about your privacy, so here's the simple truth:

  • The game has no ads and no in-app purchases.
  • I don't collect any any type of data from you.
  • Your saves, achievements, and leaderboards are handled by Google Play Services. That's Google's system, not mine. You can read their policy here: Google Privacy Policy.

Security

Since I don't collect or store data, there's no user information at risk.

Children's Privacy

The game does not collect any information from children (or anyone).

Changes

If this policy ever changes, I'll update it here.

Donations

If you choose to support me (the developer) through the OPTIONS menu in game, you'll be directed to an external donation service (BuyMeACoffee). That process is handled entirely by their own service. Smashbit or I do not collect or store any payment or personal information.
